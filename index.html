Simon and Garfunkel are an American folk-rock duo consisting of singer-songwriter Paul Simon and singer Art Garfunkel. Considered one of the best-selling artists of the 1960s, Simon and Garfunkel met in elementary school in Queens, New York in 1953 where they began writing material. The pair had their first minor success as teenagers under the "Tom and Jerry". Over the course of their career, Simon and Garfunkel won 10 Grammy Awards and drew one of the largest concert attendances in histroy at over 500,000 people.
